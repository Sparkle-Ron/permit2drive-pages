
# Privacy Policy – Permit2Drive

**Last updated:** February 2026

Permit2Drive does **not** operate any servers and does **not** collect or store your data on our systems.

## Where Your Data Lives
All data created in Permit2Drive is stored:
- **Locally on your device**, and
- **In your own Google Drive account**, *only if you choose to enable Google Drive sync*

We do not receive, copy, or have access to your data at any time.

## Information You May Store
Permit2Drive stores only the information you choose to enter, such as:
- Driver names
- Driving dates, times, and totals
- Driving conditions (day/night, weather, etc.)
- Supervisor names (if provided)

## How Your Data Is Used
- Data is used solely to provide the app’s features (logging, viewing, importing, exporting, and syncing driving records).
- No data is used for advertising, analytics, tracking, or profiling.

## Google Drive Access
If you enable Google Drive sync:
- Permit2Drive accesses **only** the specific files it creates or opens in your Google Drive.
- Access can be revoked at any time through your Google account settings.

## Data Sharing
- We do **not** sell, share, or transmit your data to third parties.
- Any file sharing or exporting is initiated entirely by you.

## Children’s Privacy
Permit2Drive may be used to track driving activity for minors. The app does not collect information directly from children beyond what a parent or guardian chooses to enter.

## Your Control
- You can delete your data from your device at any time.
- You remain in full control of any data stored in your Google Drive.
