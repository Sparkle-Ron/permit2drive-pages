About Permit2Drive

Permit2Drive is a driving log app designed to help learner drivers, families, and supervisors track supervised driving practice, review driving totals, share access when needed, and export driving log records.

Permit2Drive is provided as an organizational tool only. Driving log requirements vary by state, licensing agency, school, and program. You are responsible for confirming your current requirements with the appropriate licensing authority and making sure your records are accurate, complete, and acceptable for your situation.

Your driving log data may be stored using secure cloud services connected to your Permit2Drive account. Your device may also store some app data locally so the app can work smoothly.

Permit2Drive may allow the creator or owner of a driving log to invite other users, such as family members or supervisors, to access or contribute to that log. The owner is responsible for managing shared access and removing users who should no longer have access.

If you join a shared driving log, information you add to that log may be visible to the log owner and other authorized users. Do not add information to a shared log unless you are comfortable with authorized users of that log seeing it.

You are responsible for using the correct account and for keeping your sign-in information safe. Do not share your personal sign-in credentials with others.

Account and Data Deletion
