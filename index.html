How it works

  1. Create or sign in to your account so your driving log can be backed up and shared.
  2. Start a drive , set conditions like day or night driving, weather, road type, and notes, then save.
  3. Invite family members or supervisors when others need to help log supervised driving practice.
  4. Review totals and export when you need a clean record.

Your log is backed up and under your control

Permit2Drive uses secure cloud storage to help keep your driving log available across devices and easier to share with family members. You can delete your account and driving log data from within the app.
