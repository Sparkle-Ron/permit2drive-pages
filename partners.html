For driving schools, instructors, DMVs, and safety organizations

Help families track supervised driving with less hassle

Permit2Drive is a simple driving log app for learner drivers and their families. It helps parents and teens record practice drives, stay organized, and export clean driving logs when needed.

Get Permit2Drive View Sample Logs

What Permit2Drive helps track

Permit2Drive keeps the important driving log details together so families can review progress and export records when needed.

  • 🗓️ Date, time, and duration of each drive
  • 🌙 Daytime and nighttime driving practice
  • 🌦️ Weather, road type, and driving conditions
  • 📝 Notes about skills practiced, routes, or areas to improve
  • 👨‍👩‍👧 Driver and supervisor information
  • 📄 Exportable driving records for review or family organization

Why families need this

Many families still track supervised driving hours with paper logs, spreadsheets, notes apps, or memory. That can make it easy to lose details, forget entries, or scramble when it is time to show completed practice.

Permit2Drive gives families a simple way to record drives as they happen, keep the information organized in one place, and share access with family members or supervisors when needed.

Permit2Drive helps families:

  • Record practice drives quickly
  • Track day and night driving
  • Add weather, road type, and notes
  • Keep driver and supervisor details organized
  • Share access with family members or supervisors
  • Export clean driving records when needed

For DMVs and licensing offices

Permit2Drive is designed to help families stay organized while completing supervised driving practice.

Permit2Drive does not replace official state forms, DMV instructions, parent certifications, or licensing requirements. Families should always follow the requirements provided by their state licensing agency.

Helpful for family recordkeeping

Permit2Drive can support families who want a simple way to maintain their own driving records while following official state guidance.
