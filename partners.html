For driving schools and instructors

When families ask how to track supervised driving hours, Permit2Drive gives you an easy resource to share. Families download the app and manage their own driving records, so there is no extra setup or administration for your school.

Permit2Drive can help students and parents stay more organized between lessons while they work through the supervised driving requirements in their state. It is intended as a family recordkeeping tool, so families should always follow the official guidance from their state licensing agency.

What Permit2Drive helps track

Permit2Drive keeps the important driving log details together so families can review progress and export records when needed.

  • 🗓️ Date, time, and duration of each drive
  • 🌙 Daytime and nighttime driving practice
  • 🌦️ Weather, road type, and driving conditions
  • 📝 Notes about skills practiced, routes, or areas to improve
  • 👨‍👩‍👧 Driver and supervisor information
  • 📄 Exportable driving records for review or family organization

Why families prefer Permit2Drive

  • Free to download and start using
  • Privacy-minded: designed for family recordkeeping, not monitoring or marketing emails
  • Pause and resume drives
  • Supports multiple users, drivers, parents, and supervisors
  • Cloud-backed logs help families keep records accessible
  • Personalized background options
  • No school account, setup, or administration required
